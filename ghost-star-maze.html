Ghost's Star Maze

Guide the ghost to the door before time or moves run out!

Level 1 Stars: 0/0 Moves left: 0 Time: 0s

Arrow keys or WASD also work. Only the area near the ghost is visible!

Free and open source — MIT licensed. A friendly maze puzzle — no scares, just stars.
Hard mode: beat the clock, limited moves, and fog of war. Feel free to copy, remix, and host this anywhere.
